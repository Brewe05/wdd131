My Name Is Brent Welsford

I am a 25-year-old male from South Africa. This website is about my life and the decisions I made to serve a full-time mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I created this website as a project for my BYU assignment, but I hope it will help others in their decision to serve a mission. I hope you find the joy that the gospel brings as I did.

Enjoy my website!

Life After Mission

Returning from my mission on December 1, 2023, was not how I had imagined it...

It was strange being back and seeing that the world had moved on even though I hadn't been there. The girl I met on mission became my girlfriend but it was hard because I lived 600km away from her. I decided to make a change.

I moved to Johannesburg to be with her...

We have been together ever since, The Lord will bless you as he has blessed me, I will never regret serving my mission.

"If you have doubts, simply pray about it and follow what the Lord tells you, no matter what, and you will be blessed."
